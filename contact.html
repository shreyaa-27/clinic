Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a consultation?

You can schedule a consultation by calling us, sending a WhatsApp message, or filling out the contact form above. We'll get back to you within 24 hours to confirm your appointment.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes, we offer virtual consultations for initial discussions and follow-up appointments. Please mention your preference when booking your appointment.

What should I bring to my consultation?

Please bring a valid ID, any relevant medical records, a list of current medications, and photos or references of your desired results if applicable.

Is there a consultation fee?

Yes, we charge a consultation fee which can be applied toward your treatment if you decide to proceed. Please contact us for current consultation fees.

Post-Surgical Emergency?

If you're experiencing a medical emergency related to your surgery, please call us immediately or visit the nearest emergency room.
