Important Reminders

Emergency Contact

For any urgent concerns or complications, contact us immediately at +91 98765 43210 or visit the nearest emergency room.

Medication Compliance

Take all prescribed medications as directed. Do not stop antibiotics early, even if you feel better.

Follow-Up Appointments

Attend all scheduled follow-up appointments. These are crucial for monitoring your healing progress.

Activity Restrictions

Follow all activity restrictions carefully. Returning to normal activities too early can compromise your results.

Important Disclaimer

These audio instructions are supplementary to your written post-operative care instructions and in-person consultations. They are not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding your recovery.

If you experience any concerning symptoms or have questions about your recovery, please contact our office immediately rather than relying solely on these audio instructions.
